GENERAL

Granulated fast-acting unclogging agent for drains

DARNIL

It is used for degreasing and treating the equipment, excluding contact with foodstufs, and in other industries. It is a highly effective cleaner and degreaser for home and industrial use. Its regular use prevents sediment buildup and sewage drain choking. It is effective for cleaning paths, drives and brickwork. Composition: Caustic Soda 99% minimum. METHOD OF USE Cleaning and unblocking sewage drains. To clean drains and spouts solve 50 g of Caustic Soda in 1 litre of cold water and, avoid contact with eyes and skin, pour the entire solution down the drain. To unblock a drain solve 100 g of Caustic Soda with 1 litre of cold water, pour the solution down the drain and keep it for 10 minutes. Flush with water. If it is impossible to eliminate the blockage, it is necessary to seek professional help. General cleaning To clean paintwork resistant to alkali attack, dustbins, badly fouled areas, kennels, etc. solve 125 g of Caustic Soda in 5 litres of cold water, clean the surface with the solution, then wash it down thoroughly with cold water Cleaning paths, drives and brickwork Solve 125 g of Caustic Soda in 5 litres of cold water, pour the solution on to the area to be cleaned, scrub with a stiff brush, leave for 20 minutes, then wash it down thoroughly with cold water. Caution! Do not add water to Caustic Soda. Always add Caustic Soda to the water. Always reseal tightly after use. Keep in a dry place. Do not mix with other cleaning products. Do not mix with acids. Do not use in contact with aluminium. Avoid contact with varnishes and lacquered surfaces. Not to be used in drinking water. Identification Data of Chemical Product
